The Ground Reality

Why Pankhini Had to Exist

Uttar Pradesh has over 100 million women and girls. Yet access to mental health support, quality education, and legal awareness remains critically low — especially in semi-urban areas of NCR.

Three Flagship Programmes

Built for Real Lives.
Designed for Real Change.

Each programme addresses a specific barrier — and uses technology to remove it at scale, for both girls and women across UP and NCR.

💜
MannSakhi
मन सखी — Emotional Companion
Girls & Women

A safe, tech-enabled emotional wellbeing programme providing peer support circles, trained facilitators, and digital wellness tools — for a 14-year-old finding her voice and a 30-year-old rebuilding her life.

  • Peer support circles in schools, AWCs & communities
  • Trained MannSakhi facilitators from the community
  • Digital wellness check-ins & safe expression tools
  • Stigma-free mental health awareness campaigns
Mental Wellbeing
📚
PadhoAage
पढ़ो आगे — Study Forward
Girls & Young Women

A mobile-first EdTech platform for girls in semi-urban UP — and young women seeking skill development or career pathways. Hindi-language, low-bandwidth, built for devices real India actually uses.

  • Hindi learning platform for Class 6–12 girls
  • Vocational & skill development for young women
  • Women mentor network from professional backgrounds
  • Scholarship & higher education navigation
Education Technology
⚖️
NyayaSetu
न्याय सेतु — Bridge to Justice
Girls & Women

Technology-powered legal empowerment for girls and women who don't know their rights — or how to access them. From school harassment to domestic violence, NyayaSetu bridges every woman to justice.

  • AI-assisted legal information tool in Hindi
  • Connect to verified women lawyers & legal aid NGOs
  • Rights awareness workshops in schools & communities
  • Safe, confidential reporting & documentation tools
Legal Empowerment

How We Work

Technology Meets
Community Roots

We don't parachute in with solutions. We build with communities — using technology as an amplifier, not a replacement, for human connection and trust.

1

Identify Real Barriers

We work with Anganwadi Centres, schools, and community leaders to understand what actually stops girls from learning and women from leading.

2

Co-Design with Beneficiaries

Girls and women shape our programmes. They know their needs better than any policy document written in a city far away.

3

Deploy Tech Built for Bharat

Mobile-first, Hindi-language tools that work on basic Android phones with limited data — because real India isn't always well-connected.

4

Measure & Improve Continuously

Data-driven improvement with community accountability. Every rupee tracked, every outcome measured, every story heard.

Technology Stack

Built for Bharat — not borrowed from Silicon Valley.

Our programmes run on mobile-first Hindi platforms, working on entry-level Android devices with minimal data — reaching girls and women where they actually are.
